**Northbook = guidance, not chores.**

- Publish principles, patterns, and decisions. Store checklists in `/runbooks`.
- Prefer small, durable assets over sprawling manuals.

**Symbol rules**

- Star arms +8% thickness; top point −5%.
- Align star to book notch; nudge up 1–2 px optically.
- Corner system: book R8; star inner corners R6–R8.
- Star gap to book = 1/6 symbol width.
- Favicon: star on navy `#0F172A`, no inner cutout.

**Wordmark**

- Satoshi Medium preferred, Manrope SemiBold acceptable.
- Kerning: tighten `th`, loosen `hb`, tracking −1.5%.

**Color**

- Navy `#0F172A`, Gold `#F59E0B` (dark alt `#F8B13E`), monochrome white/black.
